# Bio-revolution & Futurology
**Original Title**: Bio-revolution & Futurology
**Authors**: Vladimir Kishinets | **Year**: 2024

## Summary
This groundbreaking work explores the imminent biological revolution that will fundamentally transform human existence through biotechnologies capable of managing human biology. Kishinets presents a compelling analysis of how emerging biotechnologies will lead to biological immortality, the elimination of aging and disease, and ultimately the transformation of Homo sapiens into a new form of intelligent life. The book examines not only the technological possibilities but also the profound social, economic, and civilizational consequences of these changes.

The book is essential reading for anyone interested in understanding the future of humanity, the philosophical implications of biotechnology, and the greatest scientific revolution in human history. Readers will gain crucial insights into the coming changes that will affect every aspect of human life within our lifetime.

## Core Insights

### The Four Directions of Biomedicine
Future biomedicine will operate through four main applications: immortation (stopping biological aging), rejuvenation (reversing age-related changes), bio-therapy (eliminating all diseases), and bio-plasty (changing physical and cognitive qualities). Unlike modern medicine that treats symptoms, this new approach addresses root causes by modifying the genome and cellular processes. The technology will work through synthetic "viruses" delivering nano-automata to cells, effectively making people biologically immortal, eternally young, disease-free, and capable of redesigning themselves.

> "Future biomedicine will have nothing to do with modern medicine chemicals and scalpels. Externally, it will look like 'infection' of a person with synthetic 'viruses' that will deliver the necessary nano-automata, nano-agents to the cells."
> _Original: "Будущая биомедицина не будет иметь ничего общего с современной медициной химикатов и скальпелей. Внешне это будет выглядеть как «заражение» человека синтетическими «вирусами», которые будут доставлять в клетки необходимые нано-автоматы, нано-агенты."_
**Why This Matters**: This represents the complete transformation of medicine from treating diseases to eliminating the very possibility of disease, fundamentally changing the human condition.

### The "New Caution" Syndrome
The mere belief in coming immortality technologies will create a "new caution" syndrome where people become extremely risk-averse to survive until these technologies arrive. This will have profound consequences: reduced willingness to work in dangerous professions, transport phobia affecting travel and tourism, and overall behavioral changes that could trigger economic crisis. The paradox is that anticipating immortality makes people value life so highly that they may avoid the very activities needed to maintain civilization until immortality arrives.

> "Growing belief of people in immortation will give rise to a syndrome of so-called 'new caution', which, rapidly spreading and increasing, will quickly grow into a General 'super-caution'."
> _Original: "Растущая вера людей в имортацию породит синдром так называемой «новой осторожности», который, быстро распространяясь и усиливаясь, очень быстро перерастет в Генеральную «супер-осторожность»."_
**Why This Matters**: The psychological effects of anticipating these technologies may be as disruptive as the technologies themselves, requiring massive automation of dangerous industries to prevent economic collapse.

### The Smenacalypse: Crisis of Transition
The period leading to universal immortality will involve a severe global crisis called the "smenacalypse" (from Russian "smena" meaning change). As workers abandon dangerous professions and people become extremely risk-averse, production will fall dramatically, leading to resource shortages, rationing systems, and the breakdown of market economies. This will force a transition to centralized, computerized economic management and full automation of hazardous industries. The crisis represents the painful birth of the new era.

> "This time, by analogy with the biblical turbulence before the onset of paradise life for the immortals, one can call, starting from the Russian word 'smena' (change), smenacalypse."
> _Original: "Это время, по аналогии с библейской бурей перед наступлением райской жизни для бессмертных, можно назвать, исходя из русского слова «смена», сменакалипсис."_
**Why This Matters**: Understanding this inevitable crisis period is crucial for preparing mitigation strategies and managing the transition to biological immortality.

### The End of Homo Sapiens
Biological modification will ultimately transform humans into "postvital" beings who are biologically perfect, immortal, asexual, and nearly identical. This represents not the extinction of humanity but its transcendence into a higher form. The reproductive instinct will be eliminated to prevent overpopulation, sexual identity will disappear, and humans will optimize their bodies to an ideal form, resulting in complete physical and psychological equality. This marks the end of the human era and the beginning of Civilization-2.

> "In order to get rid of natural shortcomings and organic 'curses' of vital life, a person will change himself with the help of technology and as a result will cease to be a man. He will disappear forever, and on the planet an heir appears, which can be called, for example, 'Sequenti sapiens', or 'next sapiens', or 'postvital'."
> _Original: "Чтобы избавиться от естественных недостатков и органических «проклятий» vitaльной жизни, человек изменит себя с помощью технологии и в результате перестанет быть человеком. Он исчезнет навсегда, а на планете появится наследник, которого можно назвать, например, «Sequenti sapiens», или «следующий сапиенс», или «поствиталя»."_
**Why This Matters**: This represents the ultimate goal and final outcome of human existence—the creation of a more perfect intelligent being through technological means.

### The Ultimate Purpose: Mega Sapiens
Even postvital beings will face the problem of accidental death over infinite timescales. The solution will be creating "Mega sapiens"—a unified, planetary-scale intelligent life form that abandons biological existence for a non-biological, highly protected form. This explains the Fermi Paradox (cosmic silence): advanced civilizations inevitably become compact, low-energy, artificial entities that are undetectable. This represents the next stage in the cosmic evolution of intelligence.

> "The solution of this grandiose task will be the historical mission of the new civilization, as a tool for the transition of planetary intelligent life to the next level of development."
> _Original: "Решение этой грандиозной задачи будет исторической миссией новой цивилизации, как инструмента для перехода планетарной разумной жизни на следующий уровень развития."_
**Why This Matters**: This provides a scientifically-grounded vision of humanity's ultimate destiny and our place in the cosmic evolution of intelligence.

## Conclusion

This book is absolutely essential reading for anyone trying to understand where humanity is heading in the coming decades. Kishinets presents a scientifically-grounded, philosophically profound vision of the biological revolution that will dwarf all previous technological and social changes in human history. While some predictions may seem extreme, the core message is undeniable: biotechnology will fundamentally transform what it means to be human.

The greatest strength of this work is its comprehensive analysis of not just the technologies but their inevitable social, economic, and philosophical consequences. Kishinets successfully connects cutting-edge scientific developments with age-old questions about the meaning of life and human destiny. Whether or not all predictions come true exactly as described, this book provides the essential framework for understanding the most important transformation in human history.

**Read this if**: You're interested in the future of humanity, biotechnology, philosophy, or want to understand the coming changes that will redefine human existence within our lifetime.

**Skip this if**: You prefer concrete immediate practical advice over long-term philosophical analysis, or if you're uncomfortable with radical predictions about human transformation.
