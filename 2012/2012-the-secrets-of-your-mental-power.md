# The Secrets Of Your Mental Power
**Authors**: William Walker Atkinson | **Year**: 2012

## Summary
This essential collection of Atkinson's writings reveals the fundamental principles of mental power and personal transformation. The book explores how our thoughts shape reality, the universal laws governing consciousness, and practical methods for developing innate mental faculties. Atkinson presents a comprehensive system for understanding human nature through both inner states and outer forms, demonstrating how the mind influences physical expression and vice versa. The work bridges psychology, metaphysics, and practical self-development, offering readers a roadmap to unlock their latent potential and achieve success through conscious mental cultivation.

Ideal for seekers of personal growth, entrepreneurs, students of human behavior, and anyone interested in the practical applications of mental science, this guide provides actionable insights for developing willpower, understanding others, and mastering the laws of thought attraction.

## Core Insights

### The Law of Vibration and Mental Attraction
Thoughts are real forces that emit vibrations, creating magnetic fields that attract corresponding circumstances and people. Just as gravity operates in the physical world, the Law of Attraction governs the mental realm. Like attracts like - positive thoughts draw positive experiences, while negative thoughts bring negative outcomes.

> "When we think we send out vibrations of a fine ethereal substance, which are as real as the vibrations manifesting light, heat, electricity, magnetism."

**Quick Take**: Your mental attitude determines what you attract. Cultivate positive thoughts to create positive circumstances.

### The Individual Will as Creative Power
The true "I" within each person is distinct from personality, body, or passing mental states. This core Individuality possesses willpower - the sword of power that can reshape both internal states and external circumstances. Success comes from expressing this authentic Individuality rather than following others.

> "The Will is a primal, original power of the 'I' which is always with it until the end. It is the force with which he rules his mental and physical kingdom."

**Quick Take**: Your authentic self contains unlimited power. Develop your ability to use will rather than seeking external authority.

### Inner State and Outer Form Connection
Human nature expresses through two complementary phases: inner mental states and outer physical forms. These constantly influence each other - your thoughts shape your expression, while consciously adopting positive physical expressions can transform internal states. Character can be read through physical manifestations.

> "Every human movement, every organic act, every volition, passion, or emotion, every intellectual process, is accompanied with atomic disturbance."

**Quick Take**: Your body and mind are interconnected. Change your posture to change your mind, or change your mind to change your body.

### The Power of Mental Images and Desire
Desire is the great motive power that incites will to action. By creating clear mental images of what we want and repeatedly focusing attention on them, we create channels for willpower to flow. The subconscious mind works to bring these images into manifestation.

> "By cultivating the Desire along certain lines, you are making channels along which the Will may flow in its rush toward expression and manifestation."

**Quick Take**: Create vivid mental pictures of your goals and fuel them with passionate desire to activate your subconscious power.

### Enthusiasm as Soul-Force
True enthusiasm comes from tapping into inner regions of consciousness, making one a magnetic center of influence. It's not superficial excitement but genuine "soul power" that inspires and attracts others. This force can be consciously developed by connecting with deeper levels of awareness.

> "A person filled with Enthusiasm seems indeed to be inspired by some power or being higher than himself – he taps on to a source of power of which he is not ordinarily conscious."

**Quick Take**: Connect with your inner source to generate authentic enthusiasm that magnetically attracts success and influences others positively.

- "Individuality is the expression of our Self – that Self which is what we mean when we say 'I'. Each of us is an Individual – an 'I' – differing from every other 'I' in the universe."
- "Earnestness and Enthusiasm are two great factors in bringing into operation these latent forces, and dormant powers of the mentality."
- "Like attracts like in the Thought World - as ye sow so shall ye reap. Birds of a feather flock together in the Thought World."
- "We are largely what we have thought ourselves into being, the balance being represented by the character of the suggestions and thought of others."
- "A passionate Desire, and an unwearied Will can perform impossibilities, or what would seem to be such, to the cold and feeble."

## Action Items

- Practice conscious posture correction: Sit erect, lift your chest, brighten your eyes, and speak in a major key to instantly elevate your mental state
- Create daily affirmations that state what you will accomplish, stating them earnestly to establish new mental attitudes
- Develop willpower through small victories: Make firm decisions and follow through consistently on small commitments
- Practice mental imaging: Spend time each day vividly picturing your goals as already accomplished
- Protect your mental atmosphere: Avoid negative influences and actively seek positive, uplifting thoughts and associations

## Questions to Consider

- What latent powers within you remain dormant because you haven't given them sufficient attention or desire to awaken?
- How might your physical expressions be reinforcing limiting mental states, and what changes could you make today?
- In what areas of your life are you following others rather than expressing your authentic Individuality?
- What clear mental images are you feeding your subconscious mind daily?
- How can you distinguish between superficial excitement and genuine soul-powered enthusiasm?

## Conclusion

Atkinson's work remains remarkably relevant for modern readers seeking practical mental development. The book's greatest strength lies in its systematic approach to understanding and applying mental laws rather than presenting vague theories. Readers will find value in the clear connection between psychological principles and tangible results, particularly the emphasis on willpower development and conscious mental cultivation.

The essential message resonates strongly: we possess far greater mental power than we realize, and by understanding the laws governing thought and consciousness, we can transform ourselves and our circumstances. While some concepts may challenge conventional thinking, the practical applications are immediately useful. The book deserves attention from anyone serious about personal development, especially those seeking a comprehensive framework that bridges scientific psychology with practical spiritual wisdom.

This isn't merely inspirational reading but a practical manual for mental development that rewards consistent application. The principles work regardless of belief - they represent universal laws of consciousness. For those willing to experiment with the techniques and persist in practice, the potential rewards extend far beyond material success to profound personal transformation.
