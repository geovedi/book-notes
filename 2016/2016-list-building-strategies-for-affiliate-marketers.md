# 2016 List Building Strategies for Affiliate Marketers
**Authors**: Anthony Ekanem | **Year**: 2016 | **Category**: Business & Finance

## Summary
This practical guide demystifies the art of email list building specifically for affiliate marketers. It presents a systematic approach to creating responsive, targeted email lists that become the cornerstone of profitable affiliate marketing campaigns. The book emphasizes that unlike random traffic, a well-nurtured email list represents a valuable asset that can generate consistent income through strategic relationship building and targeted promotions.

This guide is perfect for aspiring affiliate marketers, digital entrepreneurs, and online business owners who want to move beyond erratic traffic sources and build a sustainable income stream. Readers will gain a clear roadmap for creating high-converting squeeze pages, crafting irresistible incentive offers, and implementing automated email sequences that convert subscribers into loyal customers.

## Core Insights

### The Essential List Building Triad
Successful email marketing rests on three fundamental components that work together as a conversion system: a professional autoresponder service, a compelling squeeze page, and a high-value giveaway incentive. The autoresponder manages your subscriber relationships and automated communications, the squeeze page serves as your 24/7 lead capture machine, and the incentive provides the immediate value exchange that motivates visitors to join your list. Without all three elements working in harmony, your list building efforts will struggle to gain traction.

> "List building helps facilitate the process of converting subscribers into active customers, since once your prospect has been added to your mailing list, you are able to develop a relationship with them, which will encourage sales as well as repeat sales."

**Quick Take**: Invest in quality autoresponder services like Aweber or GetResponse from day one—they're the foundation of your email empire and worth every penny for their automation and segmentation capabilities.

### Squeeze Page Psychology and Design
A squeeze page's sole purpose is conversion—nothing else. Unlike sales pages that educate, squeeze pages must eliminate distractions and focus visitors on a single action: subscribing. The most effective pages feature attention-grabbing headlines, benefit-driven bullet points, and crystal-clear calls to action. Every element should funnel visitors toward the opt-in form, with no external links, navigation menus, or competing offers to divert their attention. The page must communicate value instantly while overcoming the natural hesitation people have about sharing their email address.

> "A squeeze page is NOT designed to sell. Its primary job is to convert traffic into confirmed subscribers and you need to keep this mind when creating your page."

**Quick Take**: Test everything—headlines, colors, button text, and layout. Even small changes can dramatically impact conversion rates, so use Google Website Optimizer to continuously improve your squeeze page performance.

### Creating Irresistible Incentive Offers
Your giveaway determines whether visitors subscribe or bounce. The most effective incentives solve immediate problems or satisfy existing desires your target audience already has. Look at what's successfully selling in your market and create a condensed, high-quality version you can offer for free. This could be a leaked chapter from a paid product, a comprehensive report, or a step-by-step tutorial guide. The key relevance and perceived value—if competitors are charging for similar information and you're giving it away free, subscription rates will soar.

> "The key to creating a successful giveaway product is in its overall relevance and existing demand. You want to make sure that your target audience is actively searching for similar information, and that it is being sold successfully in the marketplace."

**Quick Take**: Research your market's pain points thoroughly, then create an incentive that addresses the most urgent ones. Your free offer should be so valuable that subscribers would have gladly paid for it.

### Traffic Generation and Promotion Strategy
Building a list without traffic is like having a store without customers. Successful list builders employ multiple traffic channels simultaneously: article marketing establishes authority and drives organic traffic, social media profiles provide additional opt-in opportunities, and creating multiple squeeze pages for the same niche captures different audience segments. Article marketing deserves special attention—it's consistently effective for generating targeted traffic when you provide genuine value in resource boxes that compel readers to visit your squeeze page.

> "Article marketing is a very effective (and free!) marketing strategy and for many of us end up being our primary force behind generating consistent traffic to our squeeze pages."

**Quick Take**: Create a content calendar and commit to publishing 3-5 high-quality articles weekly, each targeting specific keywords related to your incentive offer. Consistency beats intensity in article marketing.

### Relationship Building Through Email Sequences
The money is in the list, but the fortune is in the follow-up. Your email sequence should balance value provision with strategic promotions, starting with immediate delivery of your promised incentive. The first 7-10 days are critical for establishing trust and setting expectations. Smart marketers begin with pure content emails, gradually introducing promotional offers as subscribers become accustomed to your communication style. Segment your lists based on subscriber behavior and interests to deliver increasingly relevant content that boosts engagement and conversion rates.

> "Essentially, you want to 'condition' your list so that they grow accustomed to receiving promotional based emails from you on a regular basis."

**Quick Take**: Create a 30-day email automation sequence before you start driving traffic, ensuring every new subscriber receives consistent value regardless of when they join your list.

## Action Items
- Set up professional autoresponder accounts with Aweber or GetResponse before creating any squeeze pages
- Research your target market's most pressing problems and create a high-value incentive that solves one of them
- Build multiple squeeze pages testing different headlines, layouts, and incentive offers
- Establish a consistent content creation schedule for article marketing (3-5 articles weekly minimum)
- Create a comprehensive 30-day email automation sequence balancing content and promotions
- Implement list segmentation from day one based on subscriber source and behavior
- Test everything—headlines, colors, button placement, and subject lines using split testing tools
- Add squeeze page opt-in forms to all social media profiles and content platforms

## Questions to Consider

- How can I segment my subscriber list to deliver increasingly personalized and relevant content?
- What balance of value vs. promotion will work best for my specific target audience?
- Which traffic generation methods align with my strengths and budget for consistent list growth?
- How can I maintain regular communication with my list without overwhelming subscribers or causing fatigue?
