# 2016-The-A-to-Z-of-Internet-Marketing
**Authors**: Anthony Ekanem | **Year**: 2016 | **Category**: Business & Finance

## Summary
"The A to Z of Internet Marketing" is a comprehensive guide that demystifies the world of online marketing for beginners and experienced entrepreneurs alike. The book covers the fundamental concepts, evolution, and practical strategies needed to succeed in digital marketing, emphasizing that internet marketing is not as complex as it appears when you understand its core principles.

This practical guide is perfect for aspiring online entrepreneurs, small business owners, and anyone looking to establish a steady income stream through digital channels. Readers will gain actionable strategies for reaching global markets, building customer relationships, and creating sustainable online businesses with minimal upfront investment.

## Core Strategies & Frameworks

### 1. The Foundation: Understanding Internet Marketing
Internet marketing encompasses all methods of promoting and selling products through online channels. The book breaks it down into two primary models:

**Ecommerce**: Direct sales to consumers through online storefronts with features like product catalogs, shopping carts, and secure payment processing. Success requires quality products, precise descriptions, and excellent customer support.

**Affiliate Marketing**: Promoting others' products for commissions through clicks or sales. This model can be lucrative but typically works best alongside other revenue streams.

> "Internet marketing is the art of advertising and marketing goods and services through the Internet."
**Quick Take**: Start with one model and expand as you gain experience and resources.

### 2. The Information-Driven Approach
The central thesis reveals that **information is the driving force of internet marketing**. Today's customers seek knowledge before making purchases, making content marketing your most powerful tool.

**Text-Based Information** includes:
- Articles on directories (Ezine Articles, Articles Base)
- Blogging on platforms like Blogger or WordPress
- Content that educates and builds credibility

**Video-Based Information** leverages:
- YouTube as the primary platform (world's 2nd largest search engine)
- Demonstrations, tutorials, and product showcases
- Visual impact that text alone cannot achieve

> "People who visit the Internet are doing so because they are looking for something. They may not be interested in buying something right away, but Internet marketing is a domain of soft sell."
**Quick Take**: Create valuable content that answers customer questions before they ask.

### 3. The Social Media Revolution
Social networking has transformed internet marketing by enabling direct engagement with target audiences. The top platforms include:

**Facebook**: Largest global reach with users in open, receptive mindsets
**Twitter**: Microblogging for real-time updates and customer engagement
**Google Plus**: Integrated social services with mobile accessibility
**Pinterest**: Visual discovery engine perfect for product showcasing
**Digg**: Content voting system for viral marketing potential

> "When people come with an open mind like that, there are chances that they will be more receptive to what you have to tell them."
**Quick Take**: Focus on platforms where your target customers spend their time.

### 4. Customer Retention Principles
The book emphasizes that keeping customers is more profitable than constantly acquiring new ones:

**The Modern Marketing Philosophy**: Customers should come to sellers, not vice versa. Create valuable content and let customers find you through search engines and social platforms.

**Value First Approach**: Provide exceptional value and support to encourage repeat business and word-of-mouth referrals.

**Community Building**: Use social networks to build communities around your brand and products.

**Support Systems**: Implement autoresponders and robust customer service to maintain relationships.

> "An Ounce of Support Is Worth a Ton of Business."
**Quick Take**: Invest in customer service before you invest in advertising.

### 5. Timeless Marketing Methods
Despite new technologies, traditional methods remain effective:

**Link Building**: Create inbound links through directories and partnerships to improve search engine rankings and drive targeted traffic.

**Article Marketing**: Write informative articles (500-800 words) for relevant directories to establish expertise and generate qualified leads.

**Blogging**: Maintain regular updates to build credibility, engage with customers, and improve search engine visibility.

> "It is all about strategizing and implementing the right techniques now."
**Quick Take**: Combine traditional and modern methods for maximum impact.

## Action Items

### Start Today
- Choose your business model (ecommerce or affiliate marketing)
- Create valuable content focused on customer education
- Build presence on 1-2 social media platforms where your customers gather
- Implement basic SEO through quality content and strategic linking

### Build Your Foundation
- Develop a professional website with clear product descriptions
- Set up customer support systems (autoresponders, FAQ pages)
- Create money-back guarantee policies to build trust
- Establish yourself as an expert through consistent content creation

### Scale Your Business
- Expand to multiple marketing channels as you gain experience
- Build an email list for ongoing customer communication
- Implement customer feedback systems to improve products and services
- Leverage viral marketing through social sharing incentives

## Questions to Consider

1. **Which internet marketing model best fits your skills and resources?** (Ecommerce for product creators, affiliate for those without products)

2. **What specific information does your target market need that you can provide?** Your content strategy should address these needs directly.

3. **How can you differentiate yourself in a crowded marketplace?** Focus on unique value propositions and exceptional customer service.

4. **What's your plan for customer retention beyond the initial sale?** Consider email marketing, community building, and ongoing support.

5. **How will you measure success and adjust your strategies?** Track metrics like website traffic, conversion rates, and customer lifetime value.

## Conclusion
"The A to Z of Internet Marketing" provides a solid foundation for anyone looking to build an online business. While some technologies mentioned may have evolved since 2016, the core principles remain timeless: provide genuine value through helpful content, build authentic relationships with customers, and focus on long-term success over quick profits.

**Is this worth your time?** Absolutely for beginners. The book simplifies complex concepts and provides actionable strategies for starting an online marketing business with minimal investment.

**Biggest reason to read it**: You'll gain the confidence and knowledge to start an online business using proven strategies that work across different industries and markets. The emphasis on value-driven marketing over aggressive sales tactics makes this a sustainable approach for long-term success.
