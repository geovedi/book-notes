# Attitudinal Healing and A Course in Miracles
**Original Title**: Attitudinal Healing en Een cursus in wonderen
**Author**: Els Thissen | **Year**: 2022 | **Category**: Spirituality & Religion 

## Summary
"Attitudinal Healing and A Course in Miracles" is a practical guide that bridges two powerful spiritual approaches to finding inner peace and navigating life's challenges. The book presents Attitudinal Healing's 12 principles and 10 group guidelines alongside the profound teachings of "A Course in Miracles," offering readers a path to transform their daily experiences from stress and conflict to peace and love.

The book is designed for anyone seeking easier ways to handle life's difficulties, especially those who feel triggered by old survival strategies and family patterns. Through practical application of forgiveness, choosing happiness over being right, and recognizing our essential wholeness, readers learn to connect with an attitude grounded in love and unity rather than fear and separation.

## Core Insights

### The Essence of Our Being is Love
The foundational principle teaches that love is not something we need to learn or earn, but what we already are. Our core essence is unconditional, impersonal love that remains untouched by external circumstances or past experiences.

> "The core of our being is love. It is our essential nature, what we are in truth."
> _Original: "De kern van ons wezen is liefde"_

**Quick Take**: When you feel disconnected from love, remember you haven't lost it—you've simply placed judgments between yourself and the awareness of what you already are.

### Health is Inner Peace, Healing is Letting Go of Fear
True health isn't the absence of symptoms but the presence of inner peace. Healing occurs when we release fear-based thinking and trust in the fundamental goodness of life and ourselves.

> "Health is inner peace, healing is letting go of fear."
> _Original: "Gezondheid is innerlijke vrede, genezen is angst laten varen"_

**Quick Take**: Your natural state is peace; any disturbance comes from thoughts that can be released rather than suppressed.

### Giving and Receiving are the Same in Essence
The spiritual law recognizes that what we give to others, we give to ourselves, and what we withhold from others, we withhold from ourselves. This principle transforms how we view generosity, service, and relationships.

> "Giving and receiving are essentially one."
> _Original: "Geven en ontvangen zijn in wezen één"_

**Quick Take**: Every act of giving is also receiving—practice seeing how your kindness to others returns to you multiplied.

### We Can Release the Past and Future
Peace comes from full presence in the now, free from regret about yesterday or anxiety about tomorrow. Each moment offers a fresh start when we choose to be fully present.

> "We can let go of the past and the future."
> _Original: "We kunnen het verleden en de toekomst loslaten"_

**Quick Take**: The only time that truly exists is now—practice bringing your full attention to this moment repeatedly throughout the day.

### We Can Choose to Be Peaceful Inside Regardless of Outside Circumstances
Inner peace is a choice, not a condition dependent on external factors. We have the power to maintain our inner equilibrium regardless of what happens around us.

> "We can consciously choose to be peaceful inside, regardless of what happens outside us."
> _Original: "We kunnen er bewust voor kiezen vanbinnen vredig te zijn, wat er ook maar buiten ons gebeurt"_

**Quick Take**: Your inner state is your responsibility and your power—choose peace first, then respond to life's challenges from that foundation.

### We See Either Love or a Call for Love in Others
Every action and word from others either expresses love or represents a cry for love when it's forgotten. This perspective transforms conflict into compassion and judgment into understanding.

> "We can always see ourselves and others as people who are either spreading love or calling for love."
> _Original: "We kunnen onszelf en anderen altijd zien als mensen die óf liefde verspreiden, óf een beroep doen op liefde"_

**Quick Take**: When someone behaves in ways you find difficult, ask yourself: "Is this love, or a cry for love?" and respond accordingly.

## Action Items

- Practice the core affirmation: "The essence of my being is love" when meeting people or feeling stressed
- When conflict arises, ask: "Do I want to be right, or do I want to be happy?"
- Start each day by choosing peace over fear in your primary relationships
- Practice seeing love or calls for love in everyone you encounter
- Release attachment to past stories and future anxieties by returning to present-moment awareness
- Join or create an Attitudinal Healing group for support in applying these principles

## Questions to Consider

- What would change in your life if you fully accepted that your core essence is love?
- Where do you consistently forget that peace is an internal choice?
- Which past stories are you telling that keep you from experiencing present-moment peace?
- How would your relationships transform if you saw all behavior as either love or a call for love?
- What patterns of judgment block you from experiencing your natural state of inner peace?

## Conclusion
"Attitudinal Healing and A Course in Miracles" offers a profound yet practical roadmap for anyone seeking inner peace in a chaotic world. The book's strength lies in its accessibility—translating deep spiritual wisdom into daily practices that anyone can apply. Els Thissen masterfully bridges two complementary spiritual traditions, creating an integrated approach that honors both the intellectual depth of "A Course in Miracles" and the practical simplicity of Attitudinal Healing.

The biggest reason to read this book is its direct approach to transformation: rather than just understanding concepts, you're invited to apply them immediately. The 12 principles serve as daily reminders of who you truly are, while the group guidelines provide community support for your journey. If you're ready to move beyond spiritual theory into lived experience of peace and love, this book provides both the roadmap and the companionship needed for the journey.
