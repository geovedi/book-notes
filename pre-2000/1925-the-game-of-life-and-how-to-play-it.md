# The Game of Life and How to Play It
**Authors**: Florence Scovel Shinn | **Year**: 1925

## Summary

"The Game of Life and How to Play It" reveals life as a spiritual game governed by universal laws that can be learned and mastered. Florence Scovel Shinn presents life not as a battle, but as a game of giving and receiving where our thoughts, words, and imaginations create our reality. The book teaches that by understanding spiritual laws—particularly the power of the spoken word, nonresistance, and love—we can consciously design our lives and manifest our deepest desires.

Written in an accessible, story-rich style, this classic self-help work shows readers how to play life's game successfully by replacing fear with faith, lack with abundance, and resistance with acceptance. It's particularly valuable for anyone feeling stuck, fearful, or uncertain about their life direction, offering practical spiritual tools for transformation.

## Core Insights

### Life is a Game of Spiritual Laws
The book's central premise is that life operates under definite spiritual laws, much like a game with rules. When we understand these laws, we can play to win rather than struggle against invisible forces. The fundamental law is "whatsoever a man soweth, that shall he also reap"—our thoughts, words, and actions return to us with mathematical precision. Shinn emphasizes that imagination is the creative faculty: "Keep thy heart (or imagination) with all diligence, for out of it are the issues of life." What we consistently image eventually manifests in our outer reality.

> "Most people consider life a battle, but it is not a battle, it is a game. It is a game, however, which cannot be played successfully without the knowledge of spiritual law."
**Quick Take**: Your imagination is the "scissors of the mind"—use it to cut out only what you want to experience.

### The Three Minds: Subconscious, Conscious, and Superconscious
Shinn explains the three departments of mind that govern our experience. The subconscious is power without direction—it carries out whatever we impress upon it. The conscious mind sees life as it appears to be and often impresses the subconscious with limitations. The superconscious mind is the God Mind within, containing perfect ideas and our divine destiny. Success comes when we align the subconscious with superconscious perfection rather than conscious appearances.

> "The subconscious, is simply power, without direction. It is like steam or electricity, and it does what it is directed to do; it has no power of induction."
**Quick Take**: Your subconscious is a faithful servant—give it the right orders by impressing it with your desired outcomes.

### The Spoken Word Creates Reality
Our words have tremendous creative power and continually make laws for ourselves. Idle words often bring disaster, while intentional words of truth create our desired reality. Shinn shares stories of people who spoke themselves into success or failure through their casual conversations. The key is to speak only words of health, wealth, love, and perfect self-expression, even when appearances contradict them.

> "By your words ye are justified and by your words ye are condemned."
**Quick Take**: Every word you speak is a creative force—choose words that bless, heal, and prosper.

### Nonresistance is Supreme Power
The law of nonresistance teaches that nothing can resist an absolutely nonresistant person. Like water that can wear away rock, nonresistance overcomes opposition through its yielding nature. When we resist situations, we give them power over us. When we bless them or "baptize them success," they lose their grip and fall away. This principle applies to people, situations, and challenges.

> "Nothing on earth can resist an absolutely nonresistant person."
**Quick Take**: Bless your enemy and you rob him of his ammunition—resistance gives problems their strength.

### The Law of Karma and Forgiveness
Karma is simply the law of cause and effect—our thoughts, words, and deeds return to us. However, Shinn reveals a higher law: forgiveness and grace. While karma operates automatically, we can transcend it through forgiveness and aligning with divine will. When we forgive others and ourselves, we neutralize karmic debt and open the way for grace to operate.

> "Christ has redeemed us from the curse of the Karmic law, and the Christ within each man is his Redeemer and Salvation from all inharmonious conditions."
**Quick Take**: Forgiveness is your spiritual eraser—it wipes out karmic patterns and opens the way for new beginnings.

### Casting the Burden on the Christ Within
When we carry burdens of worry, fear, or lack, we violate spiritual law. Shinn teaches to "cast every burden upon the Christ within" where it becomes light and is dissolved into nothingness. This isn't avoidance but transferring the problem from limited human consciousness to infinite divine intelligence. The result is immediate relief and eventual manifestation of good.

> "I cast this burden on the Christ within, and I go free!"
**Quick Take**: Your burdens become light when you place them in hands capable of handling them perfectly.

### Love Fulfills the Law
All disease and unhappiness come from violation of the law of love. Love is selfless, fearless, and the strongest magnetic force in the universe. When we send real love to others, it returns to us multiplied. Jealousy, resentment, and criticism are love's opposites and create the very situations we fear. Perfect love casteth out fear and is the fulfillment of all spiritual law.

> "Love or goodwill takes every trick."
**Quick Take**: Love is both the cause and effect of spiritual success—give it freely and receive it abundantly.

### Intuition is Divine Guidance
Intuition, or "hunches," is our unerring guide from the superconscious mind. Successful people learn to follow their intuitive leads immediately and without questioning. Intuition often comes in unexpected ways—a chance remark, a sudden impulse, a passage in a book. The key is to act on guidance rather than reason it away.

> "Intuition is a spiritual faculty and does not explain, but simply points the way."
**Quick Take**: Your hunches are divine GPS—follow them even when they don't make logical sense.

### Divine Design Ensures Perfect Self-Expression
Each person has a unique divine design—perfect self-expression that no one else can fulfill. This includes health, wealth, love, and perfect self-expression (the "square of life"). When we demand the divine design for our lives, everything adjusts to support this higher purpose. Resistance and struggle often indicate we're operating outside our divine pattern.

> "There is a place that you are to fill and no one else can fill, something you are to do, which no one else can do."
**Quick Take**: Your life has a divine blueprint—ask to see it and watch everything realign to support it.

## Action Items

- **Watch Your Words**: Begin monitoring your conversation. Eliminate negative, limiting, and fearful statements. Replace them with positive affirmations about what you want to experience.

- **Practice Nonresistance**: When faced with challenges, bless them and say, "This situation is working for my highest good." Resist the urge to fight or argue with reality.

- **Follow Your Hunches**: Act on intuitive impulses immediately, especially when they seem illogical. Practice taking "divine action" without overthinking.

- **Cast Your Burdens**: When worried or overwhelmed, use the affirmation: "I cast this burden on the Christ within, and I go free!"

- **Forgive Quickly**: Practice immediate forgiveness of yourself and others. Remember that forgiveness isn't condoning—it's freeing yourself from karmic patterns.

- **Use Affirmations Daily**: Start each day with positive declarations such as: "I give thanks for this perfect day, miracle shall follow miracle and wonders shall never cease."

- **Practice Active Faith**: Take actions that demonstrate your belief in your desired outcome, even when appearances contradict it ("dig your ditches before the rain comes").

- **Give Cheerfully**: Practice giving with love and expecting returns. The law of circulation ensures that what you give comes back multiplied.

- **Demand Divine Design**: Regularly affirm: "Infinite Spirit, open the way for the Divine Design of my life to manifest."

- **Love Unconditionally**: Send love and goodwill to everyone, especially those who challenge you. Remember that what you send out returns to you.

## Conclusion

"The Game of Life and How to Play It" remains a timeless masterpiece because it transforms abstract spiritual principles into practical, actionable wisdom. Florence Scovel Shinn's genius lies in her ability to make metaphysical laws accessible through vivid stories and clear explanations.

This book is absolutely worth your time if you're ready to take conscious control of your life experience. Its biggest strength is showing how everyday thoughts and words create your reality—making you the creative authority of your life. The principles work regardless of religious background because they operate as universal laws of consciousness.

The most valuable reason to read this book is that it gives you the "rules of the game" for creating a life of health, wealth, love, and perfect self-expression. By understanding and applying these principles, you move from being a victim of circumstance to being a conscious creator of your experience. The book doesn't just inspire—it provides a practical toolkit for transformation that has worked for millions of readers over nearly a century.

In our current age of uncertainty and rapid change, Shinn's teachings are more relevant than ever. They offer a stable foundation for navigating life's challenges while maintaining spiritual poise and creative power. This is not just a book to read once—it's a manual for living that rewards repeated study and application.
