# Knights of Today
**Original Title**: Chevaliers d'aujourd'hui
**Authors**: André Van Bosbeke | **Year**: 1987 

## Summary
"Chevaliers d'aujourd'hui" (Knights of Today) is a groundbreaking investigative exposé of secret societies and knightly orders operating in the 20th century. Van Bosbeke, with unprecedented access to insiders and former members, pulls back the curtain on organizations ranging from ancient Rosicrucian orders to modern neo-knightly fraternities. The book combines historical research with contemporary investigations to reveal how these shadowy organizations continue to influence politics, finance, and society while maintaining their ancient traditions and mystical claims.

This book will appeal to conspiracy researchers, historians, and anyone interested in the hidden networks of power that operate beneath the surface of modern society. Readers will gain insight into how ancient esoteric traditions have adapted to contemporary contexts and continue to attract followers seeking spiritual enlightenment or social status.

## Core Insights

### The Historical Evolution of Secret Societies
Van Bosbeke traces the fascinating transformation of knightly orders from medieval military organizations to modern secret societies. The book reveals how the decline of feudal warfare in the 14th century forced these orders to reinvent themselves, moving from actual military service to ceremonial and esoteric pursuits. This evolution shows a pattern of adaptation that has allowed these organizations to survive for centuries.

> "The origins of chivalry—as is increasingly supposed—are found in ancient Germanic customs."
> _Original: "Les origines de la chevalerie - comme on le suppose de plus en plus - se trouvent dans les anciennes coutumes germaniques."_

**Quick Take**: Secret societies survive by constantly adapting their purpose while maintaining their mystical allure and hierarchical structure.

### The Rosicrucian Phenomenon
The book provides an extensive examination of A.M.O.R.C. (Ancient and Mystical Order Rosae Crucis), one of the largest contemporary secret societies with approximately five million members worldwide. Van Bosbeke details their initiation rituals, historical claims (including supposed connections to ancient Egypt), and their modern business-like operations centered around San José, California.

> "A.M.O.R.C. claims to have the monopoly of the knowledge of life and death hidden from humanity."
> _Original: "A.M.O.R.C. prétend avoir le monopole de la connaissance de la vie et de la mort cachée à l'humanité."_

**Quick Take**: Modern secret societies often mix genuine mystical traditions with sophisticated marketing and organizational techniques.

### The Persistence of Templar Legacy
One of the book's most compelling sections explores the various organizations claiming descent from the original Knights Templar. Van Bosbeke documents how numerous groups compete for legitimacy and authority, often engaging in legal battles and public disputes over who represents the "true" Templar tradition.

> "They are among us" - referring to how modern Templars claim continued existence and influence.
> _Original: "Ils sont parmi nous"_

**Quick Take**: The competition between different organizations claiming the same ancient legacy reveals more about contemporary power dynamics than historical authenticity.

### Political Connections and Influence
The investigation reveals extensive connections between these secret societies and political power, including detailed documentation of relationships with European nobility, intelligence agencies, and even conspiracy networks like the Italian P2 Masonic lodge. Van Bosbeke shows how membership in these orders often serves as a networking tool for political and business elites.

**Quick Take**: Secret societies function as both spiritual organizations and networking platforms for the powerful and influential.

### The Psychology of Belonging
Through interviews with current and former members, Van Bosbeke explores why people are drawn to these organizations despite their secrecy and often questionable claims. The book reveals how the combination of ancient wisdom, exclusive membership, and the promise of special knowledge creates a powerful psychological appeal that transcends rational skepticism.

**Quick Take**: The desire for meaning, status, and secret knowledge drives people to join these organizations despite their obvious shortcomings.

## Key Takeaways
- **Skeptical Investigation**: Approach secret society claims with critical thinking while acknowledging their genuine cultural significance
- **Historical Context**: Understanding the evolution of these organizations helps explain their contemporary appeal
- **Network Analysis**: Recognize how these societies function as both spiritual and networking organizations
- **Psychological Appeal**: The combination of mystery, exclusivity, and ancient wisdom creates powerful attraction
- **Modern Adaptation**: These organizations demonstrate remarkable adaptability in changing historical contexts

## Questions to Consider
1. How do secret societies maintain their appeal in an age of information transparency and scientific rationalism?
2. What psychological needs do these organizations fulfill that mainstream institutions cannot?
3. How do these societies balance their spiritual claims with their often sophisticated business and networking operations?
4. What role do these organizations play in maintaining and reproducing elite power structures?
5. How can we distinguish between genuine spiritual traditions and elaborate schemes for status or profit?

## Conclusion
"Chevaliers d'aujourd'hui" is essential reading for anyone interested in understanding the hidden dimensions of power and spirituality in contemporary society. Van Bosbeke's investigative rigor, combined with his unprecedented access to insiders, makes this book both authoritative and fascinating. While some readers may find the sheer number of organizations and their complex interrelationships overwhelming, the book rewards careful reading with insights into how ancient traditions continue to shape modern consciousness and social structures.

The book's greatest strength lies in its balanced approach - Van Bosbeke is neither a naive believer nor a cynical debunker, but rather a careful investigator who documents both the legitimate spiritual aspirations and the obvious commercial and status-seeking aspects of these organizations. This nuanced perspective makes "Chevaliers d'aujourd'hui" a valuable resource that transcends the typical conspiracy genre and offers genuine insight into human nature and the persistence of mystery in our supposedly rational age.

Whether you're interested in history, psychology, spirituality, or the hidden networks of power, this book provides a compelling glimpse into a world that operates largely unseen, yet continues to exert influence over our collective imagination and, sometimes, our actual institutions.
