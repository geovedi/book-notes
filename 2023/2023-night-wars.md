# Night Wars
**Authors**: Graham Masterton | **Year**: 2023
**Category**: Horror & Thriller

## Summary
When newborn babies in Louisville, Kentucky begin crying uncontrollably and refusing to sleep, recently-fired journalist Sasha Smith discovers they've had their dreams stolen by dark forces seeking to unravel the universe itself. Sasha learns she's descended from a line of "Night Warriors" - guardians who defend humanity's dreams in the dream world. As Xanthys, the Time Curver, Sasha must embrace her hidden identity and join other dream-warriors in battle against the Winterwent and his forces who want to steal the universal knowledge contained in newborns' first dreams to destroy creation.

**Perfect for**: Horror fans who enjoy supernatural thrillers with unique mythology, readers who love stories about hidden magical abilities, and anyone fascinated by the intersection of dreams and reality. You'll get a fresh take on the eternal battle between good and evil set in both modern Louisville and a surreal dream world.

## Core Insights

### The Divine Battle in Dreams
The core premise reveals that an eternal cosmic struggle between good and evil plays out nightly in our dreams. Ashapola (a divine force of purity) maintains the universe through chosen warriors, while demonic forces seek to unravel creation by stealing universal knowledge from newborns' first dreams. This explains historical collapses of civilizations - they fell when dream-warriors failed to protect humanity's collective consciousness.

> "There is a dazzling force of purity in the universe, which some call God, and others call by many different names. In the world of dreams it is known as Ashapola, and it is Ashapola who sent me."

**Quick Take**: Your dreams aren't just random nonsense - they're part of an ancient cosmic defense system.

### Babies as Universal Knowledge Holders
The book presents fascinating wisdom: babies are born with complete understanding of the universe - how matter was created, the positions of stars, the meaning of existence. Their first dream contains this universal knowledge, but they forget it upon waking for their own protection. The forces of darkness want this knowledge to destroy reality itself.

> "Babies, when they're born, know everything. They know the secrets of true innocence, and the secrets of true evil. Babies know the whole meaning of human existence... In here, babies know exactly why they were born, and what their future is going to be."

**Quick Take**: Newborn babies temporarily hold the secrets of the universe in their consciousness before forgetting them for self-preservation.

### The Power of Dream Identities
Every person has both a waking identity and a dream identity, but only chosen "Night Warriors" can access their full power in dreams. Sasha discovers she's Xanthys, the Time Curver, with the ability to manipulate time-streams to alter events. These dream-warriors have special abilities and weapons tailored to their talents.

> "Reality comes in many different guises, Sasha. You assume that your waking life is real... But there is a far greater reality than the one with which you're familiar. When you come to understand who you actually are, Sasha will seem like nothing more than a character in a play, and this waking world will seem like a stage set."

**Quick Take**: Your dream self might be more powerful and real than your waking identity.

## Conclusion
**Worth your time?** Absolutely, especially if you enjoy supernatural horror with unique mythology and strong female protagonists. Masterton creates a compelling dream-world mythology that feels both fresh and timeless.

**The biggest reason to read this**: The fascinating premise that dreams are battlegrounds where warriors protect humanity's collective consciousness, combined with the innovative idea that babies temporarily hold universal knowledge. The blend of modern journalism and ancient cosmic warfare creates a unique reading experience that stays with you long after finishing the book. While some horror tropes are familiar, the dream-warrior concept and the stakes of preventing the universe from unraveling make this stand out in the supernatural thriller genre.
