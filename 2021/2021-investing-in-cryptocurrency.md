# 2021 Investing in Cryptocurrency
**Authors**: Dmitriy Olegovich Arsentev | **Year**: 2021
**Category**: Business & Finance

## Summary
This comprehensive cryptocurrency investment guide covers the full spectrum of crypto investment strategies, from basic mining and staking to advanced trading techniques like margin trading, arbitrage, and algorithmic systems. Written during a bear market period, the book emphasizes risk management and due diligence in the highly volatile cryptocurrency landscape. The author provides practical frameworks for evaluating ICO projects, setting up masternodes, and identifying legitimate opportunities in a market filled with over 8,000 different cryptocurrencies.

Perfect for both beginners and experienced investors, this guide delivers actionable insights on portfolio diversification, resource utilization, and market manipulation awareness. Readers will gain access to curated tools, platforms, and strategies that can help navigate the complex world of cryptocurrency investing while minimizing common pitfalls.

### ðŸ’° Passive Income Strategies
**Staking & Masternodes**: Generate ongoing returns without active trading by participating in network validation.

**Implementation Framework**:
1. **Staking**: Earn 5-15% annually by holding PoS tokens on exchanges like Binance, Coinbase
2. **Masternodes**: Set up servers with locked coins (requires significant capital, often $10,000+)
3. **Exchange Tokens**: Hold platform tokens to receive profit sharing
4. **Investment Programs**: Utilize exchange wallet savings features

> "The main attraction of staking for crypto investors is the ability to receive passive income. Users can receive a reward for the obtained blocks without receiving the status of a validator."

**Quick Win**: Start with small staking positions on established exchanges to learn the mechanics before scaling up.

### ðŸ›¡ï¸ Risk Awareness & Market Manipulation
**Protection**: Understanding how markets are manipulated helps protect your investments.

**Common Manipulations**:
- **Wash Trading**: Fake volume creation
- **Pump & Dump**: Artificial price inflation
- **Layering**: Large fake orders to influence sentiment
- **Stop Hunting**: Targeting retail stop losses
- **Volume Cheating**: Manipulating technical indicators

> "Large players usually always take profit positions in the region of 30-40% when trading top violas."

**Quick Win**: Use limit orders instead of market orders and avoid trading during high volatility periods.

## Action Items

- **Set up your resource toolkit**: Create accounts on CoinMarketCap, TradingView, and at least 2 ICO tracking platforms
- **Start a research routine**: Dedicate 30 minutes daily to reading crypto news from reputable sources
- **Build a diversified portfolio**: Allocate no more than 10% to any single cryptocurrency project
- **Join crypto communities**: Participate in BitcoinTalk and Telegram groups for real-time insights
- **Test with small amounts**: Before committing significant capital, test each strategy with minimal investments
- **Document your analysis**: Keep detailed records of your investment decisions and outcomes
- **Establish entry/exit rules**: Define clear criteria for when to buy and sell based on your research

## Questions to Consider

1. What is your risk tolerance, and which investment strategies align with it?

5. How will you stay updated on market developments and regulatory changes?
