# Huntsmen And Hoods
**Authors**: Laura Greenwood | **Year**: 2021
**Category**: Fantasy & Science Fiction

## Summary
Red (Aredenne) has always dreamed of becoming a Huntsman, breaking tradition in the male-dominated organization at Grimm Academy. When she finally earns her place through rigorous trials, she discovers that achieving her dream is just the beginning. She must navigate training, prove herself to skeptical colleagues, and face her ex-sweetheart Peter who betrayed her and also joined the Huntsmen.

Readers who enjoy fairy tale retellings with strong female protagonists, sweet romance, and stories about breaking gender barriers will love this book. The story combines coming-of-age elements with fantasy adventure and a gentle love story, making it perfect for fans of reimagined classics who want to see traditional roles challenged and redefined.

## Core Insights

### Breaking Barriers Through Determination
Red's journey to become the first female Huntsman in years demonstrates how unwavering determination can overcome institutional prejudice. Despite being told women don't belong in the Huntsmen, she uses her skills, confidence, and relentless spirit to prove her capability. Her success shows that tradition should never limit ambition.

> "I don't want to be a Huntsman, I'm going to be one."
**Quick Take**: Self-belief combined with demonstrated skill creates an unstoppable force for change.

### Finding Love After Betrayal
The romance between Red and Kit develops slowly and naturally, contrasting sharply with her previous relationship with Peter, who cheated on her. Their partnership built on mutual respect, professional admiration, and genuine care shows how healthy relationships emerge from shared values rather than superficial attraction.

> "He's my partner, and I need him to trust me to have my back. If that involves sharing secrets about myself, then so be it."
**Quick Take**: True partnership requires vulnerability and trust, not just proximity.

### Prophecy vs. Free Will
Like all students at Grimm Academy, Red has a prophecy she's meant to fulfillâ€”mirroring the Little Red Riding Hood tale involving a journey to her grandmother's house and dangers in the forest. However, she actively works to change or avoid her prophesied fate, suggesting that foreknowledge doesn't guarantee predetermined outcomes.

> "I refuse to shy away from my family duty because of something as small as a prophecy."
**Quick Take**: Our choices matter more than the destinies others predict for us.

### The Power of Representation
Red's success inspires other women to join the Huntsmen, showing how breaking one barrier can create opportunities for many. By the epilogue, she's recruited four more women, fundamentally changing the institution's makeup and proving that visibility matters in creating social change.

> "I wanted this before I understood what it would truly mean. That I'll become a legend. And now I know that, I want it even more."
**Quick Take**: Personal achievements have ripple effects that extend far beyond individual success.

## Conclusion
**Huntsmen And Hoods** is a delightful fairy tale retelling that successfully transforms Little Red Riding Hood into an empowering story of ambition, romance, and social change. Red's journey from determined outsider to respected Huntsman offers both entertainment and inspiration, while her sweet romance with Kit provides emotional warmth without overshadowing the main narrative.

The book is absolutely worth your time if you enjoy fairy tale retellings that challenge traditional gender roles, feature capable female protagonists, and blend fantasy elements with coming-of-age themes. At its heart, this is a story about how one person's determination can change an entire institution, making it perfect for readers who believe in the power of breaking barriers and creating new possibilities. The biggest reason to read it? Red's unwavering confidence and the satisfaction of watching her prove everyone wrong while finding love and respect along the way.
