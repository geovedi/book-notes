# Make Money With Amazon Affiliate Program
**Authors**: Juha Öörni | **Year**: 2017

## Summary
This comprehensive guide reveals how to generate income through Amazon's affiliate marketing program without requiring a personal website or blog. The book presents multiple strategies for promoting affiliate links across various platforms, including social media, forums, YouTube, and content sharing sites. It emphasizes building trust and providing genuine value to audiences while leveraging Amazon's massive product catalog and commission structure.

Perfect for beginners looking to enter affiliate marketing, side hustlers seeking additional income streams, and content creators wanting to monetize their online presence. Readers will learn practical methods to start earning commissions immediately, with or without technical skills or existing websites.

## Core Insights

### Amazon Associates: Your Entry Point to Affiliate Marketing
Amazon's affiliate program stands out as the most beginner-friendly option due to its simple application process and vast product catalog. Unlike other affiliate marketplaces that require individual product approvals, Amazon Associates gives you instant access to millions of products across every category. The program works seamlessly with non-blogging platforms and provides easy-to-use tools for link integration.

> "The Amazon Associates program works well with many non-blogging platforms. Plus, their tools make it easy to integrate your unique affiliate links into the content you share."
> _Original: "The Amazon Associates рrоgrаm wоrkѕ wеll wіth mаnу nоn-blоggіng рlаtfоrmѕ. Pluѕ, thеіr tооlѕ mаkе іt еаѕу tо іntеgrаtе уоur unіԛuе аffіlіаtе lіnkѕ іntо thе соntеnt уоu ѕhаrе."_

**Quick Take**: Start with Amazon Associates because it eliminates the complex approval processes of other affiliate networks while giving you access to products your audience actually wants.

### Reddit: The Untapped Goldmine
Reddit represents one of the most powerful platforms for affiliate marketing, with millions of daily active users. Success requires finding relevant subreddits (like /r/TheBestOfAmazon, /r/AmazonDeals, /r/AmazonUnder20) and sharing unique, interesting products rather than common items like iPhones or Samsung Galaxies. The key is building karma (at least 200 points) before posting affiliate links and following community guidelines strictly.

> "My maximum sales are from Reddit... I share my affiliate links to recommend the most interesting products to people."
> _Original: "Mу maximum ѕаlе аrе frоm rеddіt… Hоw? I ѕhаrе mу affiliate links to recommend thе mоѕt іntеrеѕtіng рrоduсtѕ tо реорlе."_

**Quick Take**: Focus on subreddit communities where users are actively looking for product recommendations, and always provide genuine value rather than just spamming links.

### Social Media Strategy Without a Website
Social media platforms offer direct access to massive audiences without website ownership costs. Facebook Pages work exceptionally well for the three most profitable niches: health, relationships, and money. The approach involves building an engaged community first, then naturally introducing relevant affiliate products that genuinely benefit your followers.

> "Choose a topic you enjoy and start building an active audience by sharing relevant information people will love."
> _Original: "Chооѕе а tоріс уоu enjoy аnd ѕtаrt buіldіng асtіvе аudіеnсе bу sharing rеlеvаnt іnfоrmаtіоn реорlе wіll lоvе."_

**Quick Take**: Create content around topics you genuinely care about, and the affiliate income will follow naturally as your community grows.

### Content-Based Promotion Methods
Creating valuable content remains the most sustainable approach to affiliate marketing. This includes writing viral eBooks, creating YouTube series, producing honest product reviews, or developing instructional content. The fundamental principle is always putting viewer value first, with financial gain as a secondary benefit.

> "The content has to be full of value for viewers. Financial gain has to be secondary to this."
> _Original: "Thе соntеnt hаѕ tо bе full of value fоr vіеwеrѕ. Fіnаnсіаl gаіn hаѕ tо bе ѕесоndаrу tо thіѕ."_

**Quick Take**: Focus on solving real problems for your audience, and they'll naturally trust your product recommendations.

## Action Items

- **Sign up for Amazon Associates**: Create your account and set up tracking IDs before implementing any strategies
- **Build platform credibility**: Establish karma on Reddit (200+ points) or grow social media following before posting affiliate links
- **Choose your niche**: Focus on health, relationships, or money niches for maximum profitability
- **Create value-first content**: Develop helpful content that naturally incorporates relevant affiliate products
- **Research subreddit communities**: Identify and participate in product recommendation subreddits that match your niche
- **Engage authentically**: Focus on genuine community participation rather than just dropping links

## Mindset for Success

The book emphasizes seven critical attitudes for affiliate marketers:
1. **Be Selective** - Your product choices directly impact your followers' trust
2. **Be Genuine** - Share products you authentically recommend, including their drawbacks
3. **Exercise Good Judgment** - Compare multiple products honestly to help buyers make informed decisions
4. **Share Your Story** - Build trust through personal experiences with products
5. **Be Open and Responsive** - Address audience questions and concerns transparently
6. **Know Your Products** - Use products yourself before recommending them
7. **Build Community** - Either create your own community or serve existing ones authentically

## Questions to Consider

- Which platform (Reddit, social media, YouTube, etc.) best matches your existing skills and interests?
- How can you provide genuine value to your chosen audience before asking for sales?
- What niche topics do you have genuine expertise or passion for?
- How will you maintain authenticity while pursuing financial goals?
- What's your plan for building trust and community before monetizing?

## Conclusion
This guide is absolutely worth your time if you're looking for practical, no-nonsense strategies to start affiliate marketing without the technical overhead of website development. The biggest strength is its focus on platform-specific tactics that work today, particularly the Reddit strategy and social media approaches. While some methods may require adaptation as platform policies change, the fundamental principles of value-first marketing and authentic engagement remain timeless. Start with one platform, master it, then expand to others – this book provides the roadmap for that journey.
