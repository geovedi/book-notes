# Getting Reviews for Amazon FBA
**Authors**: George Pain | **Year**: 2017

## Summary
This practical guide reveals how Amazon FBA sellers can legally obtain authentic product reviews while avoiding account suspension. The book explains Amazon's strict review policy changes that banned incentivized reviews and provides proven strategies to build legitimate customer feedback that drives sales and profitability. Perfect for both new and experienced Amazon sellers looking to scale their private label businesses ethically and sustainably.

Readers will learn to navigate Amazon's review ecosystem safely, understand why authentic reviews matter for marketplace success, and implement white-hat tactics to generate customer endorsements without risking their business.

## Core Insights

### Understanding Amazon's Review Policy Revolution
Amazon implemented sweeping changes to combat fake reviews, banning most forms of incentivized feedback except through their exclusive Vine program. These changes include strict customer verification, prohibiting seller-initiated review requests, and forbidding discounts in exchange for reviews. The policy aims to protect buyers from misleading information and create genuine marketplace trust.

> "Amazon has created new changes of late which are clearly reflected in Amazon review policy"
**Quick Take**: Success now requires focusing on product excellence rather than review manipulation.

### Why Reviews Are Your Most Valuable Business Asset
Reviews function as customer votes of confidence that directly impact your pricing power and sales volume. Products with strong positive reviews can command premium prices, attract more customers, and build marketplace goodwill that shields you from cut-throat competition. Essentially, reviews = business goodwill = profitability.

> "Goodwill (positive reviews) enables you to earn a premium by charging premium price"
**Quick Take**: Each authentic review is an investment in your business's long-term profitability.

### Critical Mistakes That Will Get You Banned
Never offer monetary inducements for reviews, create fake customers, use friends/family for reviews, or engage in negative reviews against competitors. Also prohibited: promotional codes for reviews, voting on your own review helpfulness, and participating in review exchange programs. These violations can permanently destroy your Amazon business.

> "The ultimate cost of making Amazon review mistake is being banned"
**Quick Take**: When in doubt, prioritize customer experience over review generation tactics.

### Legal Strategies for Organic Review Generation
Focus on creating superior products and excellent customer service that naturally inspire reviews. Use marketing inserts (gift cards, thank you notes), email follow-ups, and build customer loyalty through engagement. Drive more traffic to increase review probability while maintaining quality focus.

> "You don't have to feel victimized. If you are a genuine seller with great product to sell, this is the moment to celebrate"
**Quick Take**: Great products + great service = great reviews.

### Amazon Vine: The Only Legal Incentive Program
Amazon Vine is the exclusive platform where reviewers receive products for free in exchange for honest, unbiased feedback. Reviewers are carefully selected by Amazon based on reputation, independence, expertise, and experience. To access Vine, you typically need to become an Amazon Vendor through Vendor Central or Vendor Express.

> "Amazon Vine is the only incentivized way to get reviews for your products on Amazon"
**Quick Take**: Vine reviews are legitimate but require vendor status and intellectual property rights.

### Traffic Generation for Review Amplification
Increase your review potential through both Amazon marketplace optimization (SEO, FBA selection) and external traffic sources (social media, PPC advertising, niche websites). More qualified traffic means more customers and higher review probability. Quality traffic targeting matters more than sheer volume.

> "The probability of receiving a review increases with the number of customers who buy your products"
**Quick Take**: Traffic strategy should align with your target customer profile for best review results.

### Building Review Relationships Ethically
Network with Amazon's top-ranked reviewers, engage influential bloggers in your niche, join Facebook reviewer groups, and participate in niche forums. Create your own community through blogs and clubs. Use email follow-ups strategically after ensuring customer satisfaction, not immediately after purchase.

> "A satisfied customer will be keen on further engagements and would be more willing to honour your request"
**Quick Take**: Build genuine relationships first, request reviews second.
