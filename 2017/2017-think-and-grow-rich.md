# Think and Grow Rich - Complete Edition
**Authors**: Napoleon Hill | **Year**: 2017

## Summary
"Think and Grow Rich" is a timeless masterpiece that reveals the 13 fundamental principles for achieving extraordinary success and wealth. Based on Hill's 25 years of research studying over 25,000 people including Andrew Carnegie, Henry Ford, and Thomas Edison, this book demonstrates that thoughts truly become things when mixed with definite purpose, persistence, and burning desire. The core premise is that anyone can accumulate riches by understanding and applying these principles—regardless of their background, education, or current circumstances. The book serves as both a philosophical guide and practical blueprint for transforming mental attitudes into material wealth.

Entrepreneurs, business leaders, and anyone seeking financial independence will find this book invaluable. Readers will learn how to develop unwavering faith, master their subconscious mind, harness the power of organized planning, and overcome the six fears that hold most people back from success. The book's greatest value lies in showing how success begins with a state of mind rather than external circumstances.

## Core Insights

### Burning Desire: The Starting Point of All Achievement
Success begins with an obsessive, burning desire for your goal—not just a wish or hope, but a definite, all-consuming purpose that you're willing to stake your entire future on. Edwin C. Barnes demonstrated this when he traveled by freight train to meet Thomas Edison, declaring he would become Edison's business partner despite having no money, connections, or prospects. This unwavering desire drove him to work tirelessly until he achieved partnership and built a fortune. The key is transforming passive wants into active, definite goals that dominate your thoughts and actions.

> "Truly, 'thoughts are things,' and powerful things at that, when they are mixed with definiteness of purpose, persistence, and a BURNING DESIRE for their translation into riches, or other material objects."

**Quick Take**: Define exactly what you want, fix it in your mind, and refuse to accept any other outcome.

### Faith: Visualization of and Belief in Attainment of Desire
Faith is the head chemist of the mind that transforms thought impulses into their physical equivalent. It's developed through repeated affirmations and emotionalization of your desires. When thoughts are mixed with faith and emotion, they become powerful forces that the subconscious mind must translate into reality. The book emphasizes that faith is a state of mind that can be developed at will through the application of the 13 principles.

> "Repeatedly impress thoughts of what you desire upon your subconscious mind until those thoughts become your dominating thoughts."

**Quick Take**: Act as if you already possess what you desire, and your subconscious mind will find ways to make it reality.

### Auto-Suggestion: The Medium for Influencing the Subconscious Mind
Auto-suggestion is the bridge between the conscious and subconscious mind. Through repeated self-talk and affirmations, you can plant any desired thought into your subconscious mind, which then works continuously to bring those thoughts into reality. The key is to mix emotion and faith with your affirmations, and practice them consistently until they become automatic beliefs.

> "The subconscious mind will translate into its physical equivalent, a thought impulse of a negative or destructive nature, just as readily as it will act upon thought impulses of a positive or constructive nature."

**Quick Take**: Speak to yourself daily in positive terms, mixing emotion with your words, until your desired outcomes feel inevitable.

### Specialized Knowledge: Personal Experiences or Observations
General knowledge won't make you rich—specialized knowledge organized and applied through practical plans is what creates wealth. The key is to decide what kind of specialized knowledge you need, then acquire it through practical experience, education, or assembling a mastermind group. Henry Ford proved this by becoming incredibly wealthy despite having minimal formal education, because he specialized in organizing knowledge and attracting specialists who filled his gaps.

> "Knowledge will not attract money, unless it is organized and intelligently directed, through practical plans of action, to the definite end of accumulation of money."

**Quick Take**: Identify the specific knowledge needed for your goals, then organize and apply it through practical action plans.

### Imagination: The Workshop of the Mind
Imagination is where all plans are created and organized. There are two types: synthetic imagination (rearranging existing concepts into new combinations) and creative imagination (receiving hunches and inspiration from infinite intelligence). Great fortunes are born when ideas are developed through imagination and transformed into reality. Every great achievement began as a thought in someone's imagination.

> "Man's only limitation, within reason, lies in his development and use of his imagination."

**Quick Take**: Dedicate time to creative thinking and brainstorming, treating imagination as your personal workshop for developing wealth-building ideas.

### Organized Planning: The Crystallization of Desire into Action
No one succeeds without a practical, organized plan. Your plan should be detailed, actionable, and continuously adapted based on feedback. Most importantly, you must take immediate action on your plans and be willing to adjust them as needed while maintaining your definite purpose. The book emphasizes that successful leaders make decisions quickly and change them slowly, if at all.

> "The most intelligent man living cannot succeed in accumulating money—nor in any other undertaking—without plans which are practical and workable."

**Quick Take**: Create detailed, actionable plans for your goals and take immediate action, remaining flexible while staying focused on your ultimate objective.

### Decision: The Mastery of Procrastination
Successful people make decisions quickly and confidently, while unsuccessful people tend to make decisions slowly and change them frequently. Procrastination is the opposite of decision and is a common enemy of success. The book emphasizes that analysis of several hundred millionaires revealed that every one of them had the habit of reaching decisions promptly and changing them slowly.

> "People who fail to accumulate money, without exception, have the habit of reaching decisions, if at all, very slowly, and of changing these decisions quickly and often."

**Quick Take**: Practice making decisions quickly and confidently, avoiding the trap of endless analysis and procrastination.

### Persistence: The Sustained Effort Necessary to Induce Faith
Persistence is the sustained effort necessary to induce faith and overcome obstacles. It's based on willpower and is essential for converting desire into its monetary equivalent. Most people quit "three feet from gold" just before success, as demonstrated in the story of gold miners who stopped drilling just three feet from striking a massive vein. Persistence is developed through definiteness of purpose, desire, self-reliance, definite plans, accurate knowledge, cooperation, and willpower.

> "Before success comes in any man's life, he is sure to meet with much temporary defeat, and, perhaps, some failure. When defeat overtakes a man, the easiest and most logical thing to do is to QUIT. That is exactly what the majority of men do."

**Quick Take**: Develop the habit of continuing your efforts despite temporary setbacks and obstacles, knowing that success often comes just after the point where others give up.

### Power of the Master Mind: The Driving Force
The Master Mind is the coordination of knowledge and effort between two or more people to achieve a specific goal in a spirit of harmony. This creates a third mind that can access knowledge and inspiration beyond any individual's capacity. Andrew Carnegie attributed his entire fortune to the power of this principle, surrounding himself with specialists who formed a mastermind alliance.

> "No individual has sufficient experience, education, native ability, and knowledge to insure the accumulation of a great fortune, without the cooperation of other people."

**Quick Take**: Surround yourself with like-minded, success-oriented people who can help you achieve your goals through shared knowledge and support.

### The Mystery of Sex Transmutation
Sex transmutation is the process of converting sexual energy into other forms of creative energy and achievement. When this powerful emotion is redirected from physical expression to creative pursuits, it becomes a driving force behind genius, invention, and artistic creation. The book notes that many of history's greatest achievers were driven by this transmuted energy.

> "Sex transmutation is simple and easily explained. It means the switching of the mind from thoughts of physical expression, to thoughts of some other nature."

**Quick Take**: Channel your strongest emotions and drives into your creative and business pursuits for enhanced productivity and results.

### The Subconscious Mind: The Connecting Link
The subconscious mind is the connecting link between the finite mind of humans and infinite intelligence. It operates continuously and can be directed only through habitual thought and emotion. Your subconscious mind doesn't reason or argue—it simply acts upon the thoughts you consistently feed it, whether positive or negative. This is why controlling your dominant thoughts is crucial for success.

> "Everything which man creates, BEGINS in the form of a thought impulse. Man can create nothing which he does not first conceive in THOUGHT."

**Quick Take**: Consistently feed your subconscious mind positive thoughts and images of your desired outcomes, and it will work continuously to help you achieve them.

### The Brain: A Broadcasting and Receiving Station for Thought
The brain operates as both a broadcasting and receiving station for thought vibrations. When you "tune" your brain through positive emotions, you can pick up thoughts, ideas, and knowledge from others, as well as broadcast your own thoughts into the ether. This explains phenomena like hunches, intuition, and creative inspiration. The principle of telepathy becomes understandable through this concept.

> "The brain becomes both a broadcasting and receiving station for the vibration of thought, through the medium of the ether."

**Quick Take**: Keep your mind tuned to positive emotions to better receive creative ideas and opportunities from the collective consciousness.

### The Sixth Sense: The Door to the Temple of Wisdom
The sixth sense is the faculty through which you receive accurate knowledge without the use of your physical senses. It's often described as intuition, hunches, or creative inspiration. This sense develops gradually through the application of the other 12 principles and serves as your direct connection to infinite intelligence. Napoleon Hill described using this principle through imaginary "counselors" who provided guidance and inspiration.

> "The sixth sense is that portion of the subconscious mind which has been referred to as the Creative Imagination. It has also been referred to as the 'receiving set' through which ideas, plans, and thoughts flash into the mind."

**Quick Take**: Practice developing your intuition through meditation and by paying attention to hunches and inspiration that come to you throughout the day.

### How to Outwit the Six Ghosts of Fear
The sixth basic fear is actually an overview of the six core fears that hold people back: poverty, criticism, ill health, loss of love, old age, and death. These fears create negative states of mind that manifest as physical reality. The book provides detailed methods for recognizing and conquering these fears, emphasizing that fears are only states of mind that can be controlled and eliminated through conscious effort.

> "Man can create nothing which he does not first conceive in the form of an impulse of thought. Following this statement, comes another of still greater importance, namely, MAN'S THOUGHT IMPULSES BEGIN IMMEDIATELY TO TRANSLATE THEMSELVES INTO THEIR PHYSICAL EQUIVALENT."

**Quick Take**: Identify and systematically eliminate your fears by replacing them with faith and positive expectations of success.

## Conclusion
"Think and Grow Rich" is absolutely worth your time—it's not just about accumulating money, but about developing the mindset and habits necessary for success in any area of life. The book's greatest strength lies in its practical, actionable principles that have been tested by thousands of successful people. Hill's 25 years of research provide credible evidence that these methods work consistently across different industries and time periods.

The biggest reason to read this book is that it fundamentally changes how you think about success and possibility. Rather than believing circumstances determine your outcomes, you'll understand that your thoughts, beliefs, and actions create your reality. While some concepts may seem esoteric at first, the practical applications become clear through Hill's numerous real-world examples and step-by-step instructions.

This book isn't a get-rich-quick scheme but a comprehensive philosophy of achievement that requires dedication, persistence, and hard work. However, for those willing to apply its principles, it offers a reliable roadmap to extraordinary success that has stood the test of time for over 80 years. Whether you're seeking financial freedom, business success, or personal mastery, these principles provide the foundation for transforming your desires into reality.
