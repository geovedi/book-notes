# Stay Sexy & Don't Get Murdered
**Authors**: Karen Kilgariff, Georgia Hardstark | **Year**: 2019

## Summary
"Stay Sexy & Don't Get Murdered" is a practical, humorous, and brutally honest self-help guide from the hosts of the true crime podcast "My Favorite Murder." Through personal stories, cautionary tales, and hard-won wisdom, Kilgariff and Hardstark share lessons learned from their own struggles with addiction, trauma, terrible relationships, and personal growth. The book combines dark humor with genuine advice on self-care, setting boundaries, avoiding dangerous situations (both literal and metaphorical), and building authentic connections.

Perfect for listeners of their podcast or anyone who appreciates candid talk about mental health, recovery, and navigating life's pitfalls with humor and grace. Readers will gain practical strategies for self-protection, friendship, and personal growth.

## Action Items
- Identify your "clutch-five" friends who genuinely care about your wellbeing
- Practice saying "no" without apology when something feels wrong
- Find a therapist even if you think your problems aren't "big enough"
- Get financially independent, even if it means taking a job you don't love temporarily
- Learn to recognize when you're in emotional sinkholes and have exit strategies
- Practice direct, honest communication even when it's uncomfortable
- Listen to your intuition about people and situations

## Questions to Consider
