# Procrastination Cure
**Authors**: Tiffany Adams | **Year**: 2020

## Summary
This comprehensive 2-in-1 guide combines proven goal-setting strategies with 31 practical life hacks to help readers overcome procrastination and achieve their full potential. Written by a psychologist who transformed from a chronic procrastinator to successful professional, the book presents a seven-step goal achievement system alongside daily techniques to rewire your brain for productivity. The approach is grounded in psychological research and real-life experience, offering both long-term strategic planning and immediate actionable solutions.

Readers seeking to break free from procrastination patterns will find value in this practical blend of goal-setting methodology and habit-forming techniques. The book is particularly helpful for those who struggle with starting projects, maintaining motivation, or turning ambitious goals into achievable daily actions.

## Core Insights

### Understanding Procrastination's Root Causes

The author explains how procrastination becomes wired into our neural pathways through repeated patterns, creating a cycle that feels impossible to break. Understanding these underlying causes is crucial because "you can never achieve your goals without revealing the true cause of your procrastination."

> "Perfectionism is the voice of the oppressor, the enemy of the people. It will keep you cramped and insane your whole life."
**Quick Take**: Identify whether your procrastination stems from fear, perfectionism, or other root causes before attempting to fix it.

### The SMARTY Goal-Setting Framework
The book introduces a comprehensive goal evaluation system called SMARTY (Specific, Measurable, Attainable, Relevant, Time-Oriented, and whY). Research shows that people who write down their goals are 33% more likely to succeed, and deadlines can increase success chances by 11.5 times. This framework transforms vague wishes into concrete action plans.

> "Close your eyes and imagine how much happier your life will be when you complete that goal you've set for yourself. And remember, it's not 'if,' it's 'when.'"
**Quick Take**: Use SMARTY criteria to evaluate every goal before committing to it.

### The Accountability Partnership System
The author emphasizes that accountability partners are "the most honest and trustworthy support system" for goal achievement. These relationships provide not just monitoring but emotional support, celebration of wins, and guidance through challenges. Regular check-ins create structure and external motivation that internal willpower alone cannot sustain.

> "Accountability partners are the most honest and trustworthy support system. They will ask you how your progress is going, what your next steps are, and they'll double check that you're on track."
**Quick Take**: Choose accountability partners who will be both supportive and challenging.

### The Five-Minute Miracle Technique
This powerful productivity hack leverages the psychological principle that starting is often the hardest part. Committing to work on a task for just five minutes typically creates momentum that carries you forward. Our brains seek completion, so beginning a task creates cognitive tension that pushes us to finish.

> "This is one of the reasons why cliffhangers in books and TV shows work so well. If everything wraps up too perfectly, our brains consider the case closed and stop thinking about it."
**Quick Take**: When facing resistance, commit to just five minutes of focused work.

### The 31-Day Habit Transformation Plan
The second book provides a structured 31-day program addressing specific procrastination triggers like fear of failure, perfectionism, and self-doubt. Each day focuses on one life hack or mindset shift, building sustainable changes gradually. Research from University College London shows that habits can take 18-254 days to form, so consistency matters more than perfection.

> "According to studies done at the University College London, it can take anywhere from 18 to 254 days for a new habit to become part of your everyday routine."
**Quick Take**: Focus on daily consistency rather than immediate perfection.

## Action Items
- Write down your top 3-5 goals using the SMARTY framework

- Use the Five-Minute Miracle technique for your next procrastinated task
- Start the 31-day habit transformation plan, focusing on one day's lesson at a time

## Conclusion
This book delivers practical, psychologically-grounded strategies that work because they address procrastination's root causes rather than just symptoms. The combination of long-term goal planning with daily habit-building creates a comprehensive system for sustainable change. While the content covers familiar self-help territory, the author's personal transformation story and structured approach make it accessible and actionable.

The biggest strength is the dual focus on both strategic planning and tactical daily habits. Readers willing to commit to the 31-day challenge and implement the accountability system will likely see significant improvements in productivity and goal achievement. This book is worth your time if you're tired of generic productivity advice and want a proven framework backed by both research and real-life experience.
