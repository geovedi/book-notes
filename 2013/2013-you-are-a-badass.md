# You Are a Badass
**Author**: Jen Sincero | **Year**: 2013

## Summary
"You Are a Badass" is a refreshingly blunt and entertaining guide to stop doubting your greatness and start living an awesome life. Sincero combines personal anecdotes, edgy humor, and practical wisdom to help readers identify and change the self-sabotaging beliefs and behaviors that hold them back. The book operates on the fundamental premise that you already have everything you need within you to create the life you want—you just need to get out of your own way and own your power.

This book is perfect for anyone feeling stuck, underachieving, or knowing they're capable of more but struggling to make it happen. It's especially valuable for people who find traditional self-help too preachy or boring but still want practical strategies for personal transformation.

## Core Insights

### Understanding How You Got This Way
Your subconscious mind holds the blueprint for your life based on unfiltered information gathered in childhood. These beliefs run the show regardless of your conscious desires. When your subconscious beliefs are out of alignment with what you want consciously, you're driving with one foot on the gas and one on the brake. The first step to freedom is awareness—recognizing that you're operating from outdated programming that can be rewritten.

> "You are a victim of the rules you live by."

**Quick Take**: Your past programming doesn't have to be your future—you can change your subconscious blueprint.

### The Power of Source Energy
Everything is energy, including you. The Universe operates on vibration, and like vibrations attract like vibrations (Law of Attraction). To attract what you want, you need to raise your frequency to match what you desire. This means focusing on feeling good rather than on what's lacking. When you're connected to Source Energy and in the flow, awesome things seem to come to you effortlessly.

> "Focus on that which makes you feel good and ye shall find (attract) that which makes you feel good."

**Quick Take**: Your energy creates your reality—raise your vibration to match your desires.

### Your Brain Is Your Most Powerful Tool
Your thoughts are the most powerful tools you possess for creating your reality. The key is to change your thinking first, then evidence appears. Most people make the mistake of demanding to see evidence before believing. When you have the audacity to believe in the not-yet-seen, your reality begins to shift. Your job isn't to know the how, but to know the what and stay open to discovering the how.

> "To think what you want to think is to think the truth, regardless of appearances."

**Quick Take**: Think as if your desired reality already exists, then take inspired action.

### The Almighty Decision
You need to go from wanting to change your life to deciding to change your life. Wanting can be done passively, but deciding means jumping in all the way and doing whatever it takes. When you make a real decision, you commit fully and keep moving toward your goal regardless of obstacles. Successful people make decisions quickly and change them slowly, while unsuccessful people do the opposite.

> "Until one is committed, there is hesitancy, the chance to draw back, always ineffectiveness."

**Quick Take**: Real commitment to change precedes real change—make a firm decision and eliminate negotiation.

### Forgiveness and Gratitude as Gateway Tools
Forgiveness is for you, not the person you're forgiving. Holding onto resentment is like taking poison and waiting for your enemies to die. Forgiveness means giving up all hope for a better past. Meanwhile, gratitude connects you to Source Energy and strengthens your faith in the abundant Universe. Being grateful for what you already have and for what's coming puts you in the frequency to receive more.

> "Forgiveness means giving up all hope for a better past."

**Quick Take**: Let go of past grievances and focus on gratitude to raise your frequency and attract more good.

### Money as Energy and Relationship
Money is currency and currency is energy. Your relationship with money is as important as the actions you take to manifest it. Most people have conflicted feelings around money that keep them broke. To create wealth, you must bring yourself into energetic alignment with the money you desire to manifest. Come from abundance, not lack, and understand that the more you have, the more you have to share.

> "When you believe that everything you desire already exists, you are in a natural state of surrender."

**Quick Take**: Heal your relationship with money and see it as energy you can attract through alignment and action.

## Action Items
- Identify your limiting subconscious beliefs by examining areas of your life that aren't working
- Practice daily meditation to connect with Source Energy and raise your frequency
- Create a vision board with images of your desired reality
- Write down your goals in present tense and read them daily
- Make a decision about what you want and eliminate all negotiation with yourself
- Practice gratitude by listing 10 things you're thankful for each night
- Forgive anyone you're holding resentment against—including yourself
- Surround yourself with people who are living the way you want to live
- Take action on your goals even when you're not fully ready
- Upgrade your environment to match your desired reality

## Conclusion
"You Are a Badass" is worth your time if you're tired of playing small and ready for some tough love mixed with practical wisdom. Sincero's edgy, no-nonsense approach cuts through spiritual fluff while delivering real transformational tools. The biggest reason to read it is that it doesn't just tell you you're awesome—it shows you exactly how to own that awesomeness and put it to work in creating the life you actually want to live.

What makes this book stand out is its perfect blend of spiritual principles and street-smart practicality, delivered with humor that makes self-development actually fun. It's particularly valuable if you've tried other self-help approaches that felt too woo-woo or too conservative—Sincero strikes a balance that respects both your intelligence and your spirit.
