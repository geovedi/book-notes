## Action Items
- **Research First**: Choose ONE passive income stream and research it thoroughly before starting
- **Set Timeline**: Create specific deadlines with daily/weekly action items
- **Start Small**: Use items you already own for arbitrage, or skills you already have for digital products
- **Build Foundation**: Focus on quality and reviews early (especially important for FBA and digital products)
- **Track Everything**: Monitor your time investment vs. returns to identify most profitable activities
- **Scale Gradually**: Add new income streams only after first one becomes consistently profitable
