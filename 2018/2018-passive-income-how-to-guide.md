# Passive Income How to Guide: The Simple System to Make Money Online Within 30 Days
**Authors**: Richard Blake | **Year**: 2018

## Summary
This practical guide demystifies passive income by providing a comprehensive roadmap for generating earnings through minimal ongoing effort. The book presents over 20 different passive income strategies, ranging from traditional investments to modern digital opportunities. Blake's approach emphasizes diversification and accessibility, ensuring that readers with varying capital levels and skill sets can find suitable options to build supplemental or replacement income streams.

Ideal for beginners seeking practical ways to generate additional revenue, this guide offers concrete steps and platform recommendations for anyone looking to escape the traditional 9-to-5 work model. Readers will gain a portfolio of actionable strategies they can implement immediately with minimal upfront investment.

## Core Insights

### Investment-Based Passive Income

### Lending Club Investment
Peer-to-peer lending platforms offer significantly higher returns than traditional savings accounts, with potential annual returns around 10%. The key strategy involves spreading your investment across multiple small loans ($25 each across 100+ loans) to minimize default risk while maximizing returns through interest collection.

> "Through lending clubs, investors can earn up to 10% on yearly as interest rates. This is usually about 10 times higher or more than what would have been earned on a conventional investment."

**Quick Take**: Start with $2,500 and diversify across 100+ micro-loans to balance risk and reward.

### Real Estate Investment Trusts (Fundrise)
Fundrise democratizes real estate investing by allowing entry with as little as $500, compared to traditional property investments requiring substantial capital. The platform has delivered impressive returns, posting 9.65% - 10.88% returns in just four months of 2017, making it accessible for low-to-middle income investors seeking real estate exposure.

> "Fundrise happens to be REIT which actually is an acronym for Real Estate Investment Trust. Similar to what you have in other platforms which serve as investment trusts, they are money that professionals are managing."

**Quick Take**: Start with $500 and let professional managers handle property investment selection and management.

### Digital Creation Income

### Self-Publishing E-books
The Amazon publishing ecosystem has made it incredibly simple for authors to earn passive income. The process involves writing, editing, creating a cover, and uploading to Amazon KDP. While initial setup requires effort, successful books can generate ongoing royalties with minimal maintenance once the marketing foundation is established.

**Quick Take**: Focus on niche topics with existing demand to maximize passive earning potential.

### Udemy Course Creation
Transform your expertise into passive income by creating video courses on Udemy. The platform handles hosting, marketing, and payment processing while you earn royalties from each sale. Popular courses can generate substantial ongoing income with minimal updates required.

**Quick Take**: Start with topics you know deeply and create comprehensive, value-packed content to stand out.

### Platform-Based Passive Income

### Affiliate Marketing
Perfect for website and blog owners, affiliate marketing involves earning commissions by promoting other companies' products. This strategy requires patience and audience building but can generate significant passive income once established through recurring revenue from evergreen content.

**Quick Take**: Focus on products you genuinely use and recommend to maintain audience trust.

### Design and Digital Sales
Platforms like Amazon Merch, Cafe Press, and Etsy enable creators to earn passive income from designs and digital files without handling inventory or shipping. Upload your designs once and earn royalties indefinitely as sales continue.

> "Amazon Merch enables users to have their designs uploaded. The rest is usually handled by Amazon."

**Quick Take**: Create evergreen designs that appeal to broad audiences for consistent sales.

## Action Items
- Start one high-yield savings account or investment platform (Betterment, Fundrise, or Lending Club) with minimum initial capital
- Choose one digital creation method based on your skills (writing, design, or video) and create your first product
- Set up at least one platform-based income stream (affiliate marketing, design sales, or digital files)
- Automate your regular spending through cashback credit cards and rewards programs
- Explore rental opportunities for unused assets (property on Airbnb, storage units, or vending machines)

## Questions to Consider
- Which passive income streams align with your current skills and available capital?
- How much time can you realistically invest in upfront setup before seeing returns?
- What level of risk are you comfortable with across investment-based versus creation-based income streams?
- Which platforms and strategies offer the best long-term scalability for your financial goals?
- How can you diversify across multiple income types to build resilience against market changes?

## Conclusion
This guide provides tremendous value for anyone seeking practical, accessible ways to generate additional income beyond their primary job. Blake's strength lies in offering specific platform recommendations and concrete starting amounts for each strategy, making passive income feel achievable rather than overwhelming.

The book's biggest strength is its comprehensive coverage of both traditional investments and modern digital opportunities, ensuring readers can find options suitable for their situation. While some strategies require significant capital (real estate) or expertise (course creation), many can be started with minimal investment and scaled over time.

Worth reading for: Beginners wanting a complete overview of passive income options with actionable first steps, anyone looking to supplement their current income, and those seeking diversification strategies for their financial portfolio. The 30-day timeframe might be optimistic for substantial results, but the foundation it provides is solid for long-term wealth building.
