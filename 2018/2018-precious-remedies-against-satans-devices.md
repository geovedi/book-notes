# Precious Remedies Against Satan's Devices
**Author**: Thomas Brooks | **Year**: 1652 (2018 Edition)

## Summary
Thomas Brooks' 17th-century spiritual warfare classic systematically exposes Satan's tactics and provides biblical countermeasures for believers facing temptation, doubt, and spiritual attack. This comprehensive guide identifies over 30 distinct "devices" Satan uses to ensnare souls, each paired with practical, Christ-centered remedies that remain remarkably relevant for modern spiritual battles.

This book offers readers a strategic framework for recognizing and resisting spiritual manipulation while finding strength in biblical truth. Perfect for believers seeking deeper understanding of spiritual warfare and practical guidance for daily spiritual victory.

## Core Spiritual Devices & Remedies

### Device 1: Presenting the Bait While Hiding the Hook üé£
**Satan's Tactic**: Making sin appear attractive while completely concealing its devastating consequences. He presents the pleasure but never the pain, the delight but never the destruction.

> "Satan with ease pawns falsehoods upon us, by his golden baits, and then he leads us and leaves us in a fool's paradise."

**Spiritual Remedies**:
- Maintain maximum distance from sinful situations - don't even walk near the cliff edge
- Remember that sin is ultimately a "bitter sweet" that brings temporary pleasure but eternal regret
- Consider that sin brings the greatest losses - far outweighing any temporary gain
- Recognize sin's deceptive nature - it always promises more than it delivers

**Quick Take**: The most dangerous sins are those that look most attractive.

### Device 2: Painting Sin with Virtue's Colors üé®
**Satan's Tactic**: Disguising sin as virtue - presenting pride as cleanliness, drunkenness as social fellowship, gossip as concern, or materialism as responsible planning.

> "Satan knows that if he would present sin in its own nature and dress, the soul would rather fly from it than yield to it."

**Spiritual Remedies**:
- Remember that sin remains filthy regardless of how beautifully it's presented
- Understand that sin disguised as virtue is most dangerous because it bypasses our spiritual defenses
- Visualize how sin will appear on judgment day when all disguises are stripped away
- Consider that Christ died for the very sins Satan tries to make acceptable

**Quick Take**: The most dangerous evils are those that masquerade as good.

### Device 3: Making Repentance Seem Easy üîÑ
**Satan's Tactic**: "You can just repent later" - suggesting that future repentance makes present sin acceptable.

**Spiritual Remedies**:
- Remember that true repentance is a mighty work beyond human power alone
- Understand that genuine repentance involves thorough life transformation, not just words
- Recognize that if repentance were truly easy, not so many would be eternally lost
- View repenting as as great a work of grace as not sinning in the first place

**Quick Take**: Never sin with the expectation of easy future repentance.

### Device 4: Presenting World's Beauty to Ensnare Hearts ‚ú®
**Satan's Tactic**: Displaying the world in all its finery and beauty to capture believers' affections and draw them away from God.

> "Where one thousand are destroyed by the world's frowns‚Äîten thousand are destroyed by the world's smiles!"

**Spiritual Remedies**:
- Consider the impotency and vanity of earthly things - they cannot satisfy the soul
- Remember their uncertainty and inconstancy - wealth, fame, and beauty fade quickly
- Note how spiritually harmful worldly attachments can be
- Get better acquainted with heavenly realities that far exceed earthly treasures

**Quick Take**: The world's smiles are more dangerous than its frowns.

### Device 5: Making Souls Bold to Venture on Sin's Occasions üö™
**Satan's Tactic**: "You can walk by temptation's door without going in" - suggesting we can handle close proximity to sin without falling.

**Spiritual Remedies**:
- Remember Scripture commands avoiding ALL appearance of evil, not just the act itself
- Understand that no one achieves lasting victory over sin while regularly exposing themselves to temptation
- Follow the example of saints who fled from occasions of sin as they would hell itself

**Quick Take**: Proximity to sin increases probability of sin exponentially.

## Special Devices Against Spiritual Health

### Keeping Saints in Sad, Doubting Conditions üòî
**Satan's Tactic**: Making believers focus more on their sins than their Savior, more on their failures than God's faithfulness.

**Remedies**:
- Keep one eye on Christ's finished work and promises of forgiveness
- Look at your sins as charged to Christ's account, not just your own
- Repent for being discouraged by your sins rather than by the sins themselves
- Remember that Christ has freed you from sin's dominion, even if you still battle its presence

### Polluting Souls with Dangerous Errors ‚ùå
**Satan's Tactic**: Spreading Christ-dishonoring opinions and theological errors that undermine faith and holy living.

**Remedies**:
- Remember that an erroneous mind is as odious to God as a wicked life
- Receive truth affectionately and let it dwell plenteously in your heart
- Hate and reject all doctrines contrary to godliness, regardless of how popular they become

## Ten Special Spiritual Disciplines

1. **Walk by God's Word** - Make Scripture your ultimate authority and guide
2. **Don't Grieve the Holy Spirit** - Maintain sensitivity to divine promptings
3. **Labor for Heavenly Wisdom** - Seek God's perspective above all else
4. **Immediate Resistance** - Resist Satan's first motions, don't entertain them
5. **Be Spirit-Filled** - Stay continually filled with the Holy Spirit
6. **Keep Humble** - Remember your dependence on God's grace
7. **Maintain Spiritual Watch** - Stay alert to temptation and attack
8. **Daily Communion with God** - Maintain consistent fellowship with your Creator
9. **Draw Fresh Strength Daily** - Renew your spiritual resources each morning
10. **Be Much in Prayer** - Maintain constant communication with God

## Action Items for Spiritual Victory
- **Daily**: Start each morning with fresh strength from Christ and immediate resistance to temptation
- **Weekly**: Review potential "occasions of sin" in your life and create distance
- **Monthly**: Evaluate what "baits" Satan is using to lure you and strengthen those areas
- **Ongoing**: Keep one eye on your sins (for repentance) and one eye on your Savior (for victory)

## Questions to Consider
- What golden baits is Satan currently using to lure you into compromise?
- Which of Satan's devices have you been most vulnerable to historically?
- How can you better balance awareness of your sinfulness with confidence in Christ's righteousness?
- What "occasions of sin" do you need to flee from rather than flirt with?
- Are you more focused on your failures or God's faithfulness?

## Conclusion
**Is this worth your time?** Absolutely. While written in 17th-century language, Brooks' insights into spiritual warfare are remarkably contemporary and psychologically astute. His biblical remedies provide practical strategies that have stood the test of time.

**The biggest reason to read this book**: It equips you with both a diagnostic framework for recognizing spiritual attack and a therapeutic arsenal of Christ-centered remedies. Brooks doesn't just identify problems; he provides solutions grounded in Scripture, grace, and spiritual reality.

This work remains one of the most comprehensive and practical guides to spiritual warfare in Christian literature. Whether you're experiencing intense spiritual battles or seeking to prevent future ones, Brooks' timeless wisdom offers both immediate help and long-term spiritual strategy for living victoriously in Christ.
