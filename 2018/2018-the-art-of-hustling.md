# The Art of Hustling
**Authors**: DJ Sbu (Sbusiso Leope) | **Year**: 2018 | **Category**: Business & Finance

## Summary
"The Art of Hustling: Sell or Surrender" is a dynamic entrepreneurial guide that reframes "hustling" as a positive, ethical approach to business and life success. DJ Sbu shares his personal journey from the dusty streets of Tembisa to becoming a multi-faceted entrepreneur, demonstrating how hustling—defined as aggressive, legitimate effort toward goals—can transform dreams into reality. The book combines street-smart business strategies with spiritual principles, particularly the Law of Attraction, to create a roadmap for aspiring entrepreneurs who want to succeed while making a positive impact in their communities.

This book is perfect for aspiring entrepreneurs, young professionals, and anyone looking to develop a winning mindset in business and life. Readers will gain practical sales techniques, investment strategies, and the mental frameworks needed to overcome limitations and create lasting success.

## Core Insights

### The Positive Hustle Mindset
DJ Sbu redefines "hustling" not as illegal activities, but as aggressive, legitimate effort toward achieving goals. It's about working with purpose, resilience, and creativity to turn obstacles into opportunities. The hustle mindset includes being solutions-driven rather than complaining, thinking creatively, and never accepting "no" as the final answer.

> "Working very hard, aggressively, to make money or to achieve any goal you might have. Not taking 'no' for an answer type of attitude, in a legal way of course."

**Quick Take**: Hustling is about purposeful aggression in pursuing your goals—legally and ethically.

### The Law of Attraction for Entrepreneurs
The book emphasizes that "we become what we think about" and provides practical techniques for reprogramming the subconscious mind. This includes visualization, positive affirmations, and creating mental movies of success. Your subconscious mind cannot distinguish between imagination and reality, making visualization a powerful tool for manifestation.

> "Your reality follows your imagination. That's why Albert Einstein once said: 'Imagination is more important than knowledge.'"

**Quick Take**: Reprogram your mind by consistently visualizing your goals as already achieved—your subconscious will work to make them reality.

### The 5 Steps to a Sale
DJ Sbu presents his proven sales framework: Introduction (with eye contact, smile, firm handshake), Presentation (explain value proposition), Short Story (keep it brief), Close (ask commit questions), and Rehash (upsell strategically). This system works for selling products, services, ideas, or even yourself in interviews.

> "I think of this as the ABC of Hustling: Always Be Closing. Commit them to a sale without giving them the option of saying no."

**Quick Take**: Master these five steps and you can sell anything to anyone—from street corners to boardrooms.

### Time is the New Currency
Everyone has the same 24 hours, but successful people use time differently. The book teaches time management as a core entrepreneurial skill, emphasizing that time wasted can never be recovered. If you're on time for a meeting, you're already late—arrive early and leave late.

> "Time is the new currency... Every single one of us has 24 hours in a day. No more and no less. The people you look up to have the same 24 hours that you have—they just use their time wisely."

**Quick Take**: Treat time as your most valuable asset—invest it wisely in activities that move you toward your goals.

### Investment and Wealth Building
The book introduces Robert Kiyosaki's Cashflow Quadrant (Employee, Self-Employed, Business, Investor) as a roadmap for wealth creation. True wealth comes when your money works for you through investments in assets that generate passive income. Focus on acquiring assets, not liabilities.

> "The ultimate position that you want to put yourself in is the 'I' = INVESTOR. That's when your money is working for you. That's how rich people keep getting richer."

**Quick Take**: Build wealth by moving from employee to investor—let your money work harder than you do.

## Action Items

- **Reprogram your mind**: Create positive affirmations in present tense ("I am successful," "I am prosperous") and repeat them daily for at least 21 days
- **Master the 5-step sales process**: Practice the Introduction-Presentation-Story-Close-Rehash framework in all your business interactions
- **Invest in assets**: Focus on acquiring things that put money back in your pocket, not liabilities that take money out
- **Practice effective time management**: Arrive early for meetings, use time productively, and treat every minute as valuable
- **Start hustling early**: Don't let age be a barrier—whether you're young or older, start pursuing your passion now

## Questions to Consider

1. What limiting beliefs about yourself or your abilities have been holding you back, and how can you reprogram them?
2. Are you focusing more on acquiring assets or liabilities in your current financial decisions?
3. How can you apply the 5-step sales framework to your current career or business challenges?
4. What would you attempt if you knew you could not fail?
5. Are you using your 24 hours each day as effectively as the most successful people you admire?

## Conclusion
"The Art of Hustling" is worth your time if you're looking for a no-nonsense, street-smart guide to entrepreneurship that combines practical business strategies with spiritual principles. DJ Sbu's authentic voice and real-life examples make the concepts accessible and actionable. The biggest reason to read this book is its emphasis on ethical hustling—succeeding while making a positive impact in your community.

What sets this book apart is its South African perspective and focus on social entrepreneurship. DJ Sbu demonstrates that you can build wealth while uplifting others, challenging the notion that business must be purely profit-driven. The blend of sales techniques, investment wisdom, and mindset training provides a comprehensive foundation for entrepreneurial success.
